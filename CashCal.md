# Privacy Policy for CashCal

**Last updated: December 25, 2024**

## Introduction

SolE8023 ("we", "us", or "our") operates the CashCal mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data Stored Locally

CashCal stores all your financial data **locally on your device**. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal financial information on external servers. The following data is stored only on your device:

- Transaction records (income and expenses)
- Budget settings and categories
- Receipt images (if you use the receipt scanning feature)

### Permissions We Request

The App requests the following permissions:

- **Camera**: Used for scanning receipts. Images are processed and stored locally on your device only.
- **Storage/Media**: Used to save and access receipt images on your device.
- **Internet**: Required for displaying advertisements.
- **Network State**: Used to check network connectivity for ad loading.

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our App. AdMob may collect and use data for personalized advertising. For more information on how Google collects and uses data, please visit:

- [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google's Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/).

## Data Security

Your financial data is stored locally on your device using encrypted database storage. We do not have access to your personal financial information. However, we recommend:

- Keeping your device secure with a PIN or biometric lock
- Regularly backing up your device
- Being cautious when granting app permissions

## Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

### 로컬 저장 데이터

CashCal은 모든 금융 데이터를 **사용자의 기기에 로컬로 저장**합니다. 당사는 사용자의 개인 금융 정보를 외부 서버로 수집, 전송 또는 저장하지 않습니다. 다음 데이터는 사용자의 기기에만 저장됩니다:

- 거래 내역 (수입 및 지출)
- 예산 설정 및 카테고리
- 영수증 이미지 (영수증 스캔 기능 사용 시)

### 요청하는 권한

앱은 다음 권한을 요청합니다:

- **카메라**: 영수증 스캔에 사용됩니다. 이미지는 사용자의 기기에서만 처리되고 저장됩니다.
- **저장소/미디어**: 영수증 이미지를 기기에 저장하고 접근하는 데 사용됩니다.
- **인터넷**: 광고 표시에 필요합니다.
- **네트워크 상태**: 광고 로딩을 위한 네트워크 연결 확인에 사용됩니다.
