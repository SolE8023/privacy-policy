# Privacy Policy for Clozet

**Last updated: January 12, 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Clozet ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application Clozet (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Clothing item data**: Photos, categories, colors, seasons, brands, prices, and notes you enter about your clothing items
- **Outfit data**: Outfit combinations and names you create
- **Wear records**: Daily wear logs and associated notes
- **Trip plans**: Travel destinations, dates, and packing lists you create

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device information**: Device type, operating system version
- **App usage data**: Feature usage patterns, crash reports
- **Advertising ID**: For personalized advertising purposes

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Store your wardrobe data locally on your device
- Display personalized advertisements
- Analyze app usage to improve our services
- Diagnose and fix technical issues

## Data Storage

**All your personal wardrobe data (clothing items, outfits, wear records, trip plans) is stored locally on your device only.** We do not upload or store this data on external servers.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

### Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data for personalized advertising.

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [How Google uses data from apps](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

### Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. This helps us improve the user experience.

- [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

### Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic information to improve app stability.

- [Firebase Crashlytics Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

## Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

- **Camera**: To take photos of your clothing items
- **Storage/Photos**: To save and access clothing item photos
- **Internet**: To display advertisements and send analytics/crash reports
- **Network State**: To check network connectivity

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. Since your wardrobe data is stored locally on your device, the security of this data depends on your device's security settings.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
